About the Library

Is there anyone among you who has never been to a library? Hardly anyone!! Even if you haven't been to one, at least you are familiar with the idea of a Library.

Russell Davies gives a clear view of the power a Library can possess in his lines:

"Doctor Who: You want weapons? We're in a library. Books are the best weapon in the world. This room's the greatest arsenal we could have. Arm yourself!"

- Russell T. Davies (from Tooth and Claw in Season 2)

If there is anything you want to arm yourself with, for a period infinite, it is the weapon of Knowledge; & where better to find this weapon than in a Library?? We believe that our readers should be able to visit any part of the knowledge universe, comfortably sitting in our Library. We want them to have access to any piece of information that might strike their thoughts. So here we have embibed all arenas of knowledge, from business to fiction & technical to spiritual, we cover it all. For:

“What a school thinks about its library is a measure of what it feels about education.”

-Harold Howe

Our vision is to keep our potential readers interested in our Knowledge Box and to satiate their thirst for words! Signing off with the hope that you keep our Library UNSAFE!!

“If your library is not "unsafe," it probably isn't doing its job.”

- John Berry
